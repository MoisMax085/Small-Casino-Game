## How to Play

1. Tell the game how much money you’ve got.  
2. Pick your game:  
   - **Blackjack** 🃏  
   - **Roulette** 🎡  
3. Place your bet.  
4. Play and see if luck is on your side! ✨

---

## Game Rules

### 🃏 Blackjack

- Dealer hides 1 card. You get 2, dealer gets 2.  
- **Winning:**  
  - You get blackjack, dealer doesn’t → **You win!** 🏆  
  - Both get blackjack → **Draw** 🤝  
  - Dealer gets blackjack, you don’t → **You lose** 💀  
- If no blackjack:  
  - **Keep your cards** or **draw one more**.  
  - Dealer draws until 17–21.  
  - Dealer busts → **You win!** 🎉

### 🎡 Roulette

- Place your bet.  
- Pick a range.  
- Choose your lucky number and spin.  
- Hit the number → **Big win!** 💰
